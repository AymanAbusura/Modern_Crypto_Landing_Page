2. Is cryptocurrency a good investment?

You should get into cryptocurrencies with a realistic approach—all investments, including crypto, carry some level of risk. The risk in crypto relates to the volatility of the digital assets. You will see crypto rise from out of nowhere and plummet without warning. This volatility is what makes it a good trading opportunity if you know what you’re doing. Crypto helps you diversify your portfolio, but how heavy you would want to invest in this market depends on you.
